# Two-Sided Verification Job Boards - Competitive Analysis

**Focus:** Platforms that vet BOTH employers and candidates
**Date:** November 1, 2025

---

## Executive Summary

**The Question:** What job boards verify both employers AND job applicants?

**The Answer:** Very few! Most job boards vet employers OR candidates, but rarely both. The platforms that do dual-vetting operate as premium "talent marketplaces" rather than traditional job boards, and they charge significant fees.

**Key Finding:** The dual-vetting model is powerful but rare, creating a massive opportunity for RJRP to democratize this approach with a free tier.

---

## The Dual-Vetting Landscape

### **Why Dual-Vetting is Rare:**

Most job boards operate on a "spray and pray" model:
- **Indeed/LinkedIn:** Anyone can post, anyone can apply (minimal verification)
- **ZipRecruiter:** Employers pay, candidates are free (employer-side verification only)
- **Traditional job boards:** Focus on volume, not quality

### **The Premium Dual-Vetting Model:**

A small number of platforms vet both sides, creating curated marketplaces:
- Higher quality matches
- Better conversion rates (application ‚Üí hire)
- Premium pricing (expensive for employers)
- Exclusive positioning ("top 3% of talent")

## The Economics of Dual-Vetting

### **Why Dual-Vetting is Expensive:**

1. **Human Review Costs Money**
   - Toptal: $500-$1000/candidate to vet (hours of expert time)
   - FlexJobs: $50-$100/job to vet (human review)

2. **Low Acceptance Rates = Wasted Effort**
   - Toptal: 97% of applicants rejected (lots of review work for nothing)
   - Hired: 90% rejected

3. **Ongoing Monitoring**
   - Quality maintenance
   - Fraud detection
   - Performance tracking

### **Why Premium Platforms Charge High Fees:**

**Toptal charges $60-200/hr because:**
- Extensive vetting costs money
- Limited supply (only 3% pass)
- High-value talent commands premium
- Brand positioning as "elite"

**Hired charges $15k-20k per hire because:**
- Vetting infrastructure is expensive
- Quality guarantee (if hire doesn't work out, they help replace)
- Faster time-to-hire (worth premium)
- Candidate quality reduces risk

### **The Free Model Challenge:**

**Can RJRP offer dual-vetting for free?**

**Yes, if:**
1. **Light-touch vetting** (verification vs. assessment)
   - Email/phone confirmation: $0
   - Manual job review: 5 min/job = minimal cost
   - Automated fraud detection: low cost

2. **Community moderation** (user reports, ratings)
   - Users flag spam/fake jobs
   - Employer ratings
   - Applicant ratings

3. **Future monetization** (freemium model)
   - Free tier: basic vetting
   - Premium: deeper vetting, verified badges, priority support

**Bottom Line:** RJRP can offer Toptal-style concept at Craigslist-style price by using lighter-touch vetting + community moderation.
