Getting Started

1Create Your Account

Sign up as an employer using your work email or Google account. It only takes a minute.

2Verify Your Identity

Complete our verification process to unlock job posting. This helps maintain the quality and trust our platform is known for.

Verification levels:

  • Basic - Email verified
  • Enhanced - Email + Phone verified
  • Complete - Email + Phone + Company info verified
3Post Your First Job

Once verified, you can post jobs immediately. Include clear job details to attract the right candidates.

4Review Applications

Manage all your applications from your dashboard. Download resumes, review cover letters, and connect with candidates.

Writing Great Job Posts

A well-written job post attracts better candidates. Here are our tips:

Do
  • Use a clear, specific job title
  • Include salary or salary range
  • List actual day-to-day responsibilities
  • Be specific about required vs. preferred qualifications
  • Mention remote/hybrid/on-site status
  • Include your company culture highlights
Avoid
  • Vague titles like "Superstar Wanted"
  • "Competitive salary" without specifics
  • Unrealistic requirement lists
  • Requiring 5+ years for "entry-level"
  • Copy-pasting generic descriptions
  • Leaving out location information

Managing Your Jobs

Job Expiration

Jobs automatically expire after 60 days to keep our listings fresh and relevant. You'll see expiration badges on your dashboard:

  • Expiring Soon - Less than 14 days remaining
  • Expired - Job is no longer visible to candidates
Refreshing Jobs

Still hiring? Click the Refresh button on your dashboard to extend your job for another 60 days. This also bumps your listing in search results.

Bulk Upload

Hiring for multiple positions? Use our bulk upload feature to post many jobs at once using a CSV file. Access it from your employer dashboard.

Our Commitment to You

When you post on Real Jobs, Real People, you're joining a community committed to respectful hiring practices. We ask that you:

  • Respond to applicants - Even a brief "no" is better than silence
  • Post real opportunities - Only list jobs you're actively hiring for
  • Be transparent - Share salary ranges and job requirements honestly
  • Respect candidates' time - Keep interview processes reasonable

Together, we can make hiring better for everyone.
