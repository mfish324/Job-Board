Privacy Policy

Last updated: {% now "F d, Y" %}

Your Privacy is Our Priority

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

Information We Collect
For Job Seekers:
  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Resume and cover letters (only shared with employers you apply to)
  • Job application history (private to you)
For Employers:
  • Company name and contact information
  • Job postings (publicly visible)
  • Applicant information (only for jobs you posted)
How We Protect Your Data
  • Encrypted passwords - We never store passwords in plain text
  • Secure sessions - Automatic logout after inactivity
  • Limited access - Employers only see applicants for their own jobs
  • No selling data - We never sell your information to third parties
  • HTTPS encryption - All data is encrypted in transit
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of email notifications
Data Sharing

We only share your information when:

  • You apply for a job (your application is shared with that employer only)
  • Required by law (legal obligations)
  • You explicitly give permission

We NEVER:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with marketers
  • Make your email public
  • Share your applications with other employers
Contact Us

If you have any privacy concerns or questions, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@realjobsrealpeople.net
