About Real Jobs, Real People

Restoring dignity and humanity to the job search

Our Story

Real Jobs, Real People was born from a simple observation: the job market is broken. Ghost jobs that were never meant to be filled. Applicants who pour their hearts into applications only to be ghosted by recruiters. A system that treats job seekers as data points rather than human beings.

We've experienced it ourselves. We've watched friends and family go through the demoralizing cycle of applying, hoping, and hearing nothing back. The frustration. The self-doubt. The despair.

We believe deeply in the dignity of work. Each of us has an inherent desire to contribute to our communities, to use our skills and talents to make a difference. A job isn't just a paycheck—it's purpose, identity, and connection. The current job search system undermines all of that, and we decided to do something about it.

Why We're Different

Real Jobs Only

Every job posting represents a genuine opportunity. No ghost jobs, no fake listings.

Real People

We verify both employers and job seekers to ensure authentic connections.

Respectful Process

We encourage employers to communicate with applicants, even when the answer is no.

Human-Centered

We built this platform with job seekers' dignity in mind, not just metrics.

Our Mission

To create a job marketplace where every posting is real, every applicant is respected, and the hiring process honors the dignity of work and the humanity of all involved.

A Fresh Perspective

We come from education, not HR. We've spent decades teaching—helping young people discover their potential, solve problems, and prepare for their futures. That background gives us a different lens on the job market.

We see job seekers not as applicants to be processed, but as people with unique talents looking for the right opportunity to contribute. And we see employers not as gatekeepers, but as community members looking for the right people to help them succeed. Our job is simply to help them find each other.

Join Us

Whether you're looking for your next opportunity or searching for great talent, we're here to help make the process more human.
